1) The Basys trust workflow

Basys Analytics connects to your systems (identity, endpoint, cloud, ticketing, backups) and continuously collects control signals. Those signals become evidence packs: structured bundles with timestamps, change history, exceptions, and remediation notes. Evidence packs can be exported for audits, shared with stakeholders, or used for internal governance.

2) Evidence packs & control mapping

  • Control mapping: each signal is mapped to controls (e.g., ISO 27001, NIS2, BSI, DORA-ready mapping).
  • Traceability: configuration snapshots and change logs show what changed and when.
  • Exceptions: explicit risk acceptance with expiry dates keeps decisions auditable.
  • Export: standardized PDFs/JSON for audits and vendor questionnaires.

3) Why a token

BTrust Token is the utility layer that makes the ecosystem measurable and composable. It is used for platform access, feature unlocks, partner credits, and optional trust attestations. The token also enables incentive programs that reward verified contributions (integrations, validated evidence improvements, partner services).
